About Us

Who is Airpaz?

Airpaz, one of Asia Pacific's leading online travel agents (OTA), provides comprehensive services to meet every traveler’s needs and connects people worldwide. Our flight and hotel booking search platform enhances travelers’ booking experiences with numerous flight and accommodation options, affordable prices, exclusive offers, various local languages, reliable payment options in many currencies, and 24/7 assistance.

Our Journey

Airpaz’s journey started when we noticed the growing interest in air travel and established the company in 2011. We believe traveling is the key to connecting people worldwide regardless of distance and language, and that’s why we make it our mission to empower people to travel.
